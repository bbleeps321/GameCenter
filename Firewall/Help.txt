Description:
Prevent the wall of fire from falling to the ground.

How to play Firewall:
Left - Move towards the left
Right - Move towards the right
Space - Fire basic shot
A - Fire bomb
S - Fire pierce

*********************Weapons************************

Basic Shot
Effect: Destroys one brick
Speed: Average
Cost: 0pts

Bomb
Effect: Destroys all bricks in a 1 unit radius
Speed: Slow
Cost: 500pts

Pierce
Effect: Destroys all consecutive bricks in the same column
Speed: Fast
Cost: 1200pts

NOTE: Shots can hit another shot, neutralizing each other
