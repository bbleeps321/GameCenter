Description:
Try to hit the ball into past the opponent while 
he is trying to do the same to you. Play up to 
best of nine.

How to play Pong:
W - Move paddle 1 (on the left) upwards
S - Move paddle 1 (on the left) downwards
Shift - Stop paddle 1 (on the left)'s movement

Up - Move paddle 2 (on the right) upwards
Down - Move paddle 2 (on the right) downwards
Enter - Stop paddle 2 (on the right)'s movement
