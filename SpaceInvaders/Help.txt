Description:
Destroy as many monsters as possible while saving
energy and avoiding their attacks.

How to play Space Invaders:
Up - Move ship upwards
Down - Move ship downwards
Left - Move ship towards the left
Right - Move ship towards the right
Space - Fire laser
A - Fire bomb

***********************Ships***********************
Normal: Basic ship
Shields: 200
Cluster Bombs: 5
Speed: Average

Speeder: Extremely fast, light ship
Shields = 150
Cluster Bombs: 3
Speed: Very fast

Bomber: Very slow, heavily armed ship
Shields = 300
Cluster Bombs: 10
Speed: Very slow
