Description:
Avoid the many chasers for as long as 
possible while they slowly multiply.

How to play Avoider:
Move the mouse to control the avoider (circle)
Simply click with the mouse to begin

*********************Chasers**************************
Chaser: The basic chaser with no special traits
Color: Red
Ability: none

RandomChaser: A Chaser that will occasionally change 
              its direction and speed
Color: Blue
Ability: Random directional change

TrickyChaser: A Chaser that is normally slow but accelerates
              when you move near it
Color: Yellow
Ability: Accelerates towards you when you get too close
